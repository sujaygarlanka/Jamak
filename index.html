Purpose

Turn your smartphone into a laptop for an affordable price.
Through a non-profit that provides scholarship money to low-income engineering students in India, we noticed a barrier in their education. When asked if they needed additional money outside of the scholarship, most responded by saying a laptop would be far more useful to them than money. They were doing all their school work on their smartphones - zoom classes, reading textbooks, submitting homework, all things meant to be done on a laptop, not a smartphone.
We believe we can help these students along with others by leveraging the power of their smartphones to create a laptop experience for under $100. We can do this by providing a laptop shell that scales and mirrors the content of the smartphone. So anything that can be done on a smartphone can be done on our device. Turn your smartphone into a laptop for an affordable price.

Electronics Design - November 14th - December 4th
Based on our research, we put together a simple design illustrated above.
The data in our design flows from the trackpad and keyboard to the phone to control it via USB-B protocol and from the phone to the LCD screen via Miracast wireless protocol. Using USB-B and Miracast allows us to support multiple phone brands, microUSB, and multiple Android versions.
4
Physical Design - December 5th - 20th
We built the physical casing from scratch by joining a local Makerspace and using a combination of laser cutting, screws, glue and spray paint to put together our POC. A small selection from our many design iterations is shown below.
